Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate Pro?

After purchase, you receive a license key. In VS Code, run Launchpad: Activate Pro and paste your key. Activation takes one click.

Does Pro phone home?

License validation happens once at activation and re-checks every 24 hours. The extension itself sends zero telemetry — all classification runs offline.

How many machines can I use?

Each license key activates on up to 3 machines. Deactivate a machine from VS Code to free a slot. Lifetime licenses include unlimited reactivations.

Can I try before buying?

Absolutely. The free version includes all 195 intents, compound commands, customs, macros, trace mode, and typo tolerance. Pro just adds power-user tools.

What if I cancel?

Canceling stops future charges. Your Pro features stay active until the current billing period ends, then the extension reverts to the free tier seamlessly.
